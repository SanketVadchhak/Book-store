How It Works

            

                Your daily dose of exercise 
in 3 simple steps
            

            

                

                    
01

                    

                        Sign Up
                    

                    

                        Start your journey by creating an account. Fill in your details and set up your profile to begin accessing personalized resources and mock interviews.
                    

                

                

                    

                        
                    

                

            

            

                

                    
02

                    
Practice Mock Interviews

                    

                        Engage in simulated interviews with tailored questions. Get real-time feedback on your performance to identify strengths and areas for improvement.
                    

                

                

                    

                        
                    

                

            

            

                

                    
03

                    
Track Your Progress

                    

                        Monitor your growth with our intuitive progress tracking system. Review your past performances and receive customized recommendations to enhance your skills.
